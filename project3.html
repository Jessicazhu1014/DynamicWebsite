Media: Cannon Camera

Time: Oct,2024

Both of these photos were taken by me in Chinatown and they perfectly illustrate the different styles of different corners of Chinatown. I think Chinatown is a magical place full of cultural characteristics, in this fashionable and modern New York full of Western modern culture can produce such a piece of Chinese culture gathering, let me deeply realize the impact of cultural collision to people's lives!
